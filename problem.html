Key Farming Challenges

Understanding the real problems faced by women farmers was the first step in designing this project. AgriShe AR focuses on solving practical issues that affect planting decisions and daily farming activities.

🌿 Space Planning

Many users cannot accurately estimate the spacing required between plants. This often leads to overcrowding, poor sunlight distribution and unhealthy plant growth.

🌱 Crop Selection

Selecting the wrong crop for a small garden or farm area can reduce productivity. Users often struggle to decide which plant is suitable for the available space.

💧 Plant Care Guidance

Beginners may not know proper watering schedules, fertilizer needs or seasonal care instructions, which can affect the health of the plants.

Why this matters: These challenges reduce confidence, waste resources and make farming more difficult for women who manage agricultural tasks daily.
