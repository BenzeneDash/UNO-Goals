Key Benefits

AgriShe AR provides practical advantages that make farming simpler, smarter and more accessible for women farmers and home gardeners.

📏 Efficient Space Use

Users can understand how much area each plant needs and make better use of limited space.

🌱 Smart Decisions

Visual crop planning helps users choose the right plants before real planting begins.

⏱ Time Efficiency

The application reduces trial-and-error and speeds up planning.

💧 Resource Saving

Better planning reduces waste of seeds, water and farming effort.

👩‍🌾 Farmer Empowerment

Technology makes farming easier for women with limited technical knowledge.

📈 Better Growth

Correct placement improves plant health and productivity.

Future Enhancements

The system can be improved further by adding advanced smart farming features.

🤖 AI Detection

Future versions can identify plant diseases using AI image analysis.

🗣 Voice Support

Local language voice assistance can help users who are less comfortable with reading.

🌦 Weather Alerts

Weather updates can improve planting and protection decisions.

Project Summary

AgriShe AR is an Augmented Reality based farming support system that helps women farmers solve important problems such as space planning, crop selection and plant care. By combining agriculture with mobile technology, this project creates a simple and innovative solution that can improve farming confidence and productivity.
