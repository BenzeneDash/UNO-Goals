Innovative AR Solution

AgriShe AR uses Augmented Reality to convert a smartphone into a smart farming assistant. The system helps users visualize crops, select suitable plants and receive care guidance before planting begins.

📱 AR Placement

Users can point their mobile camera toward an empty garden or farming area and virtually place selected plants in real space before actual planting.

🌱 Crop Recommendation

The system helps users choose suitable plants based on available area, plant size and growing conditions.

💧 Care Assistance

The application provides watering reminders, maintenance suggestions and basic crop care instructions for healthier growth.

Main Advantage: Instead of guessing plant arrangement, users can make accurate farming decisions using a simple smartphone.

Why AR is Important

Traditional farming apps can only provide text information. AgriShe AR improves this by allowing users to see plants directly in their real environment. This creates a more practical and interactive planning experience.

This makes the solution easier to understand, especially for users who may not have technical farming knowledge.
